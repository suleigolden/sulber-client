Sulber — On-Demand Exterior Home & Car Services

Sulber is an on-demand services marketplace that connects homeowners with trusted, verified local providers for exterior home and vehicle services—delivered directly to the customer’s driveway, parking lot, or residence.

Sulber is designed for convenience, reliability, and seasonal balance. Customers can book services in minutes, track providers in real time, pay securely, and rate their experience. Service providers can register on the platform, verify their identity and insurance, list their services, manage availability, accept jobs, and get paid—without needing to find customers themselves.

Sulber operates across Canada, the United States, and Europe, supporting multiple currencies, regional compliance, and scalable expansion.

What Sulber Offers

Sulber focuses on on-site, exterior services that people need regularly but prefer not to do themselves.

Available Services

Driveway Mobile Car Washing
Professional car washing performed at the customer’s home or location.

Parking-Lot Car Cleaning
On-site vehicle cleaning for residential or commercial parking lots.

House Window Cleaning
Residential window cleaning for exterior and interior surfaces.

Driveway Snow Shoveling (Seasonal)
Snow and ice removal during winter months for safe access and mobility.

These services are intentionally selected to:

Be location-based

Be seasonal yet complementary

Allow providers to earn year-round

Enable easy bundling and recurring bookings

How It Works
For Customers

Choose a service and location

Select a time or request instant service

Get matched with nearby providers using GPS and availability

Track provider arrival in real time

Pay securely through the app

View before/after photos and leave ratings

For Service Providers

Register and complete identity and insurance verification

Choose which services to offer

Set pricing, availability, and service radius

Receive job requests instantly

Track earnings and payouts

Build reputation through ratings and reviews

Two-sided marketplace (customers & providers)

Real-time notifications (email, SMS, push, in-app)

Scalable infrastructure for international expansion
